Several editing pages are available according to the element selected in the tree:

  • the editing page of the general information appears if the selection comprises the global header of a soundfont (displaying the name of the soundfont),
  • the editing page of the samples appears if one or more samples are selected,
  • the editing page of instruments appears if one or more instruments are selected,
  • the editing page of presets appears if one or more presets are selected.
Otherwise, the software icon is displayed.
